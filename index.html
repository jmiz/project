The New York Times

Sure, they're a little slow to report on most things and you're guaranteed to see a "new" article published once a week about the latest neighborhood in Brooklyn that's become unaffordable or how [insert numnber] minutes of exercise a week is all you really need, but that doesn't stop me from loving the Gray Lady.

Smitten Kitchen

Unpretentious food blog with a focus on easy-to-follow recipes with ingredients you can find everywhere. Also, pictures of cute kids.

Gothamist

Sometimes obnoxious articles, but really the best way to keep up with local news.

artnet Magazine

Go to source for art world news. I used to write for their magazine before they fired everyone, ceased publication, and then started back up again.
